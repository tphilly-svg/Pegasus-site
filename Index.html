Why Shop at Pegasus?

Pegasus Sporting Goods is New Jersey’s premier soccer specialty shop, serving players, fans, and families since 1981. We stock the latest cleats, jerseys, and equipment from the brands and clubs you love.

  • Huge selection of soccer cleats for firm ground, turf, and indoor surfaces.
  • Official jerseys and apparel from European clubs, national teams, and more.
  • Local experts who help you find the perfect fit and gear for your level and position.
  • Family-run shop with deep roots in Newark’s soccer community.
