Why Shop at Pegasus?

Pegasus Sporting Goods is New Jersey’s premier soccer specialty shop, rooted in Ironbound's Portuguese community. Serving players, fans, and families since 1981. We stock the latest cleats, jerseys, and equipment from the brands and clubs you love.

  • Vast selection of soccer cleats for firm ground, turf, and indoor surfaces.
  • Official jerseys and apparel from European clubs, national teams, and more.
Designed for football culture.
A blend of street, stadium, and Portuguese matchday energy, in one local shop.
