Why Shop at Pegasus?

Pegasus Sporting Goods is New Jersey’s premier soccer specialty shop, rooted in Newark’s Portuguese community and serving players, fans, and families since 1981. We stock the latest cleats, jerseys, and equipment from the brands and clubs you love.

  • Huge selection of soccer cleats for firm ground, turf, and indoor surfaces.
  • Official jerseys and apparel from European clubs, national teams, and more.
  • Local experts who help you find the perfect fit and gear for your level and position.
  • Family-run shop with deep roots in Newark’s soccer and futsal culture.

What You’ll Find in Store

Visit Pegasus for soccer gear you can see, feel, and try on before you play. We carry options for youth and adults, beginners and competitive players.

Cleats & Footwear

Youth and adult cleats in firm ground, turf, and indoor options from brands like Nike, adidas, Puma, and more.

Portuguese & European Jerseys

Show your colors with jerseys from Benfica, Sporting, Porto and other top European clubs, plus national team gear for match day.

Balls & Equipment

Match and training balls, shin guards, goalie gloves, socks, bags, and other essentials for practice and game day.
